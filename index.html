Privacy Policy for Screw Jammed

Last updated: July 7, 2025

1. Introduction

We at Screw Jammed take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique identifiers (such as IDFA or GAID).
  • Location: Approximate location based on IP for regional services and ad targeting.
  • Language Settings: To support localized experiences.
  • Behavioral Data: In-game interactions, ad views, gameplay events.

5. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

6. Your Rights

Our game is not intended for children under 13. If we become aware of having collected data from a child without parental consent, we will delete it.
